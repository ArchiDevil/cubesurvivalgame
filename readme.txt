Islands World (Tech demo D3D11) 0.0.3.229
used:
    Direct3D 11
    DirectInput 8
    MyGUI 3.3.2
    freetype2
    tinyxml2
    jsoncpp

Keys:
V           - turn on/off wireframe
C           - open crafting menu
I           - open inventory menu
RMB click   - move player
RMB drag    - move camera around player
Mouse wheel - zoom in/out
